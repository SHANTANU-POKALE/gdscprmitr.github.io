Why join us?

Google Developer Student Clubs(GDSC) are community groups for college and university students interested in Google Developer technologies.GDSCs are open to any student, ranging from novice developers who are just starting to advanced developers who want to further their skills. The program helps bridge the gap between theory and practice in understanding the intersections between social groups, technology, and user behavior.GDSCs are hosted in 106 countries around the world, and students can participate in events organized by multiple chapters, but if they decide to dedicate themselves to become a GDSC Lead or Core Team Member, they will be officially assigned to one chapter.
