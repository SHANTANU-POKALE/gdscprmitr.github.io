Why join us?

Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) are community groups for college and university students interested in Google Developer technologies. GDSCs are open to any student, from novice to advanced developers, helping bridge the gap between theory and practice. Hosted in 106 countries, students can participate in multiple chapters, but those dedicating themselves as a GDSC Lead or Core Team Member will be officially assigned to one chapter.
